About Us

MBA CHAI WALA is an iconic brand that serves garma-garam chai & finger licking snacks with a tinge of inspiration that encourages people to forge their own identities.

MBA CHAI WALA’s greatest victory has been receiving an immense amount of love & support from all around the country.
